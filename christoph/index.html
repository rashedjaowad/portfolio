About the Summit

One week.
Many worlds.

Originally founded in 2015 in Chiang Mai — the world capital of remote work and digital nomadism — Nomad Summit has inspired countless digital nomads worldwide.

We bring together the best of all worlds: a dynamic conference packed with inspiring talks, a hands-on Buildathon where ideas meet action, and Nomad Week — a full week of 40+ community-led side events.

For those driven to build — whether it is a business, a skillset, a community, or a life of freedom.

What We Offer

Three pillars.
One week.

01
🎤

Conference

Two packed days of high-energy keynotes, panels, and workshops led by successful founders, operators, and investors at the 5-star Shangri-La Hotel in Chiang Mai.

02
⚡

Buildathon

A hands-on hackathon for builders. Mixed teams form, combine skills, and create something remarkable in a fast-paced, inspiring environment — then pitch to a jury.

03
🌏

Nomad Week

A full week of 50+ community-hosted events across 20+ venues: workshops, coworking days, hikes, dinners, and more. You bring the idea — we help you make it happen.
